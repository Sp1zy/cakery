Crafting Sweet Memories from the Heart of Sweden

What started as a simple passion for blending local organic ingredients into home-baked treats has evolved into Sweden's premier boutique cakery design studio.

At Munamii Cakery, we believe that a cake shouldn't just be a centerpiece; it should be an unforgettable sensory experience. Every layer is meticulously baked from scratch, every frosting wave is sculpted by hand, and every design is tailored explicitly to tell your personal celebratory story.

Artisan Sourcing

Bespoke Excellence

Every single cake undergoes an absolute, custom architectural design review before leaving our kitchen doors.
